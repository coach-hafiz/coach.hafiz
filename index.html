About

Coach Hafiz, a certified running coach based in Singapore, brings over a decade of experience to the running world. Specialising in in-person sprint coaching and online training plans, Coach Hafiz is dedicated to helping distance runners of all ages and levels achieve their goals. With certifications from IAAF (now World Athletics) and USATF, Coach Hafiz's structured programs are tailored to each individual, ensuring personal growth and success. As the business evolved during the COVID-19 pandemic, Coach Hafiz expanded services to include online coaching, connecting with long-distance runners globally.

Services

  • SprintEdge: Face-to-face sprint coaching (100m, 200m, 400m, 4x100m relay)
  • RunRemote: Online coaching for 800m to marathon runners worldwide
  • PacerPlans: Training plans for disciplined distance runners (no coaching included)
